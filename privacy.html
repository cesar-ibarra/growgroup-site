Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

OVERVIEW

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

GrowGroup ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our devotional accountability app.

GrowGroup is available in the United States and Canada. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

What We Collect

We collect the following information when you use GrowGroup:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Profile photo: If you choose to upload one from your photo library.
  • Devotional content: Reflections and notes you choose to share with your group.
  • Usage data: Streak counts, devotional dates, and progress milestones.
  • Group data: Your cell group membership and group code.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, or any sensitive personal information beyond what is listed above.

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide and improve the GrowGroup experience.
  • Display your profile and progress to your group members.
  • Track your devotional streak and milestones.
  • Send you notifications you have opted into.
  • Allow group leaders to view group accountability statistics.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

DATA STORAGE

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Servers are located in the United States.

Profile photos are stored in Supabase Storage with secure access controls.

YOUR RIGHTS

Your Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Edit your profile: Update your name and photo at any time from the Profile tab.
  • Delete your account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from Profile → Delete Account. This action is immediate and irreversible.
  • Leave a group: Remove yourself from a cell group without deleting your account or history.

When you delete your account, all your data — including devotionals, streaks, milestones, and profile information — is permanently removed from our servers.

PHOTOS

Photo Library Access

GrowGroup requests access to your photo library only when you choose to upload a profile photo. We do not access your photos without your explicit action. You can deny this permission at any time in your iPhone Settings.

SUBSCRIPTIONS

Subscriptions & Payments

Subscription payments are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. GrowGroup does not have access to your payment information or credit card details. All billing is managed by Apple.

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone's App Store settings.

CHILDREN

Children's Privacy

GrowGroup is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

UPDATES

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of GrowGroup after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
